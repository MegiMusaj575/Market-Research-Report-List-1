<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) heaters are a type of heating device that utilizes ceramic materials with a positive temperature coefficient of resistance. As temperature rises, their electrical resistance increases, which effectively limits current flow and prevents overheating. This characteristic makes PTC heaters both safe and energy-efficient, making them ideal for a variety of applications, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial heating.</p><p>The Positive Temperature Coefficient Heaters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.
