<p><p>The kidney stone retrieval device market encompasses several key types: Lithotripters, Stone Removal Devices, and Ureteral Stents. Lithotripters utilize shock waves to disintegrate stones into smaller fragments for easier passage. Stone Removal Devices are employed in procedures to extract stones from the urinary tract, often using minimally invasive techniques. Ureteral Stents facilitate urine flow when stones obstruct the ureter, preventing pain and complications. Together, these devices play a crucial role in managing kidney stones and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
