<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle starter market comprises two main types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starters rely on a mechanical connection for activation, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters use an electromagnetic solenoid to engage the starter motor, which enhances efficiency and allows for more complex vehicle ignition systems. Both types cater to different automotive applications, with direct control favored for basic models and electromagnetic control preferred for advanced systems requiring improved operational control and performance.</p></p>
