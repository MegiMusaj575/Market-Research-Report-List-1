<p><p>Ready-to-use Therapeutic Food (RUTF) is designed to treat severe malnutrition, particularly in children. The market comprises three primary types: solid, paste, and drinkable forms. Solid RUTF includes energy-dense, nutrient-rich bars or biscuits that are easy to store and consume. Paste RUTF is a peanut-based, ready-to-eat product that is generally preferred for infants and young children due to its palatability. Drinkable RUTF is a fortified liquid option, often used in clinical settings, providing a convenient alternative for those with swallowing difficulties. Each form targets different needs and consumption preferences.</p></p>
