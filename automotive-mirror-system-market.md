<p><p>The automotive mirror system market is divided into two main types: interior and exterior mirrors. Exterior mirrors, mounted on the vehicle's outer sides, improve side and rear visibility while often incorporating technologies such as heating, blind-spot monitoring, and cameras for advanced driver assistance systems. Both types are essential for safety, comfort, and functionality in modern vehicles.</p></p>
