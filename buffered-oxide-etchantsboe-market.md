<p><p>Buffered Oxide Etchants (BOE) are specialized etching solutions used in semiconductor fabrication to selectively remove silicon dioxide layers from silicon wafers. The two primary market types, BOE 6:1 and BOE 7:1, denote the ratios of ammonium fluoride to hydrofluoric acid in the etching formula. BOE 6:1 is typically used for applications requiring faster etching with a more aggressive approach, while BOE 7:1 provides a gentler etch, offering better control for delicate structures and finer features in microelectronics.</p></p>
