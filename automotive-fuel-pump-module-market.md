<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Pump Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Fuel Pump Module</li><li>Mechanical Fuel Pump Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel pump module market comprises two main types: electric fuel pump modules and mechanical fuel pump modules. Electric fuel pump modules are widely used in modern vehicles, featuring efficient operation and are often integral to fuel injection systems. They use electric motors to pump fuel from the tank to the engine, providing precise fuel delivery. Mechanical fuel pump modules, on the other hand, rely on engine vacuum or an offset camshaft for operation, common in older vehicles and simpler engine designs.</p></p>
