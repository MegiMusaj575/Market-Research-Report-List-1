<p><p>The Chronic Total Occlusion (CTO) devices market includes several key types essential for treating blocked arteries. Micro catheters are flexible tubes used to navigate complex vascular pathways. Guide wires assist in positioning and delivering other devices through narrowed arteries. Crossing devices enable the crossing of occlusions to restore blood flow, while re-entry devices facilitate navigating back into the true lumen of the artery after unsuccessful attempts. Together, these devices enhance the effectiveness of coronary and peripheral interventions.</p></p>
