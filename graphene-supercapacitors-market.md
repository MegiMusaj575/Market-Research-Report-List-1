<p><strong>The Graphene Supercapacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Graphene-only Based Supercapacitors</li><li>Hybrid Graphene Supercapacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphene supercapacitors market comprises two main types: graphene-only based supercapacitors and hybrid graphene supercapacitors. Graphene-only based supercapacitors utilize pure graphene materials, offering high electrical conductivity and energy density but may face limitations in power density and charge/discharge rates. In contrast, hybrid graphene supercapacitors combine graphene with other materials, such as metal oxides or conducting polymers, enhancing energy and power density while maintaining stability and efficiency. This combination allows for versatile applications in various energy storage systems.</p></p>
