<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Gate Operator market is primarily segmented into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical operators utilize gears and motors to open and close gates, offering reliability and ease of maintenance. They are ideal for lighter gates and residential applications. In contrast, hydraulic operators use pressurized fluid to control gate movement, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications, such as commercial and industrial sites. Each type serves specific needs, balancing power, speed, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
