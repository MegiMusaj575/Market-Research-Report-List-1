<p><p>Desktop virtualization in manufacturing enables companies to manage and deliver desktop environments over a network. The hosted virtual desktop (HVD) model offers personalized environments for individual users, facilitating secure and flexible access. In contrast, the hosted shared desktop (HSD) approach allows multiple users to share a single desktop environment, optimizing resource usage and reducing costs. Other market types may include hybrid models and local virtual desktops, which combine elements of both HVD and HSD, catering to diverse organizational needs.</p></p>
