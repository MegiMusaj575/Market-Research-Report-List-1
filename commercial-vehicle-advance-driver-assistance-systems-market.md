Blind Spot Detection (BSD) alerts drivers of vehicles in their blind spots, enhancing safety during lane changes. Forward Collision Warning (FCW) provides warnings of potential collisions, while Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS) alerts drivers when they drift from their lane. Park Assist System (PAS) aids in parking, and other technologies enhance overall vehicle safety and driver convenience.</p></p>
