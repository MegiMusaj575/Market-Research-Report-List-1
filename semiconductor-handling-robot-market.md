<p><p>The semiconductor handling robot market can be categorized into two primary types: vacuum robotic and atmospheric robotic systems. Vacuum robotic systems operate in controlled environments to prevent contamination, making them ideal for delicate semiconductor materials and processes. In contrast, atmospheric robotic systems function in ambient conditions, suitable for less sensitive applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing efficiency and precision in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to varying requirements in cleanliness, handling capabilities, and operational complexity.</p></p>
