<p><p>The industrial handwheels market comprises three main types: spoked handwheels, solid handwheels, and arm handwheels. Spoked handwheels feature a series of spokes radiating from a central hub, providing a lightweight yet sturdy design, ideal for manual operation. Solid handwheels offer enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy machinery applications. Arm handwheels incorporate an extended handle or arm for easier operation, enhancing leverage and control. Each type serves distinct purposes in various industrial settings, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
