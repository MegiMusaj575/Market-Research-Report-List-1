<p><p>The Continuous Heating Furnace market comprises different types, notably Rotary-Hearth and Straight-Chamber furnaces. Rotary-Hearth furnaces feature a rotating platform that allows for uniform heating and efficient material processing, ideal for high-volume applications. In contrast, Straight-Chamber furnaces provide a stationary chamber that maintains consistent temperatures, suitable for batch processing and smaller production runs. Both types cater to various industries, including metal processing and ceramics, focusing on energy efficiency, precision heating, and quality output in continuous operations.</p></p>
