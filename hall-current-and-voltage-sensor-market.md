<p><p>The Hall Current and Voltage Sensor market is categorized into Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage proportional to the magnetic field, allowing for precise readings of current or voltage levels. In contrast, Digital Output Hall Sensors deliver discrete signals, often utilizing binary coding to indicate specific thresholds. These sensors are essential in various applications, including automotive, industrial automation, and renewable energy, enhancing measurements and improving system accuracy.</p></p>
