<p><ul><li>Type 1 Fume Hoods</li><li>Type 2 Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Kitchen Fume Hoods market is categorized mainly into two types: Type 1 and Type 2 fume hoods. Type 1 fume hoods are designed for cooking equipment that emits grease-laden vapors, incorporating filters to manage flammable and combustible particles. In contrast, Type 2 fume hoods are used for applications that produce steam or heat but do not produce grease, primarily managing moisture and odors without grease filtration. Both types ensure proper ventilation and safety in commercial kitchens.</p></p>
