<p><p>The linear damper market consists of two primary types: adjustable linear dampers and non-adjustable linear dampers. Adjustable linear dampers allow users to modify the damping characteristics according to specific requirements, providing versatility for various applications. In contrast, non-adjustable linear dampers offer fixed damping capabilities, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for applications where precise control is not necessary. Both types are essential in managing motion and vibrations in mechanical systems across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
