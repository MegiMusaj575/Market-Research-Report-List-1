<p><p>The automobile interior fasteners market comprises two main types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and screws, provide strong, adjustable connections, making them ideal for securing components like seats and dashboards. Non-threaded fasteners, which include clips, rivets, and push fittings, offer quick assembly and disassembly, enhancing manufacturing efficiency and reducing weight. Both types are essential for ensuring the stability and safety of interior assemblies while meeting aesthetic and functional requirements in vehicle design.</p></p>
